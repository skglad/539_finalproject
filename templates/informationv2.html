Welcome to the Book Finder App!

Within this app, you can search through our database of books.

You can search from over 2,000 of the top rated books from GoodReads.com to find books by a specific author, search for a book's cover image, or find a specific search by term. We will be working to expand the database of books and to add more search parameters to help you find your next good read.

If you are searching for a specific author, and want a list of books written by this author:
Search for books by author


If you are looking for a list of books with a certain search term in the title:
Search for books by search term


If you know the title of a book and want to see an image of the bookcover:
Search for bookcover by title
